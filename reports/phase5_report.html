GRPO — Group Relative Policy Optimization

Schulman et al. 2017 (PPO); Shariatmadar et al. (DeepWaveRL)
$$A_g = \frac{R_g - \mu_G}{\sigma_G}$$

$R_g$: 第 $g$ 个采样的 scalar reward。$\mu_G, \sigma_G$: 组内 $G=32$ 个采样的均值和标准差。
局限: 多个 reward 分量需事先手动融合为单一标量。

DAPO — Decoupled Advantage PPO

Schulman et al. 2017 (PPO) — multi-reward independent normalization
$$A = \sum_i w_i \cdot \mathrm{normalize}(R_i)$$

每个 reward 分量 $R_i$ 独立全局标准化（减均值除标准差），然后加权求和。
改进: 解耦了多 reward 分量，但标准化粒度是全局的，丢失了组内相对排序信息。

GDPO — Group Decoupled Policy Optimization 当前

Dong et al., 2025, arXiv:2601.05242
$$A = \mathrm{batch\_norm}\!\left(\sum_i w_i \cdot \mathrm{group\_standardize}(R_i)\right)$$ $$\mathrm{group\_standardize}(R_i) = \frac{R_i - \mu_{G,i}}{\sigma_{G,i} + \epsilon}$$

每个 reward 分量 $R_i$ 在组内 $G$ 个采样上独立标准化，然后加权求和，最后 batch 全局标准化。
核心优势: 保留组内"哪个采样更好"的相对信号。即使 Wasserstein ($\sim -5000$) 和 Contrastive ($\sim 0.5$) 量级差 $10^4$ 倍，GDPO 自动处理。

▸ 7. Individual Reward Functions — Complete Reference

1. L2 Waveform Misfit Single

Tarantola 1984
$$R = -\|p_{\mathrm{pred}} - p_{\mathrm{obs}}\|^2$$

Per-group sum over all time samples, shots, receivers. 对振幅误差和 cycle-skipping 敏感。

2. L1+L2 Misfit Single

Sign-preserving log L1 + L2
$$R = -\big(\mathrm{L1}(\mathrm{sign\_log}(p_{\mathrm{pred}}), \mathrm{sign\_log}(p_{\mathrm{obs}})) + \mathrm{L2}(\cdots)\big)$$

3. Wasserstein — CDF-based Time-Domain W₁ Single Simplified OT

Métivier et al. 2016; Wei et al. 2026
$$d^+[t] = |d[t]|, \quad p[t] = \frac{d^+[t]}{\sum d^+[t]}, \quad C[t] = \sum_{\tau \le t} p[\tau]$$ $$W_1 = \sum_t |C_{\mathrm{pred}}[t] - C_{\mathrm{obs}}[t]|, \quad R = -\sum_{\mathrm{traces}} W_1$$

This is a simplified 1D CDF-based W₁, not the full Kantorovich OT. Uses abs() for non-negativity and L1 of CDF differences. See Section 8 for the standard Métivier W₂ formulation.

4. Contrastive Single

Phase IV design
$$R = 0.5 \cdot \mathrm{spec\_sim}(p_{\mathrm{pred}}, p_{\mathrm{obs}}) + 0.5 \cdot \max_\tau \mathrm{CC}(p_{\mathrm{pred}}, p_{\mathrm{obs}}; \tau)$$

5. NCC_zero Single

Liu et al. 2017, GJI
$$\mathrm{ncc}_0 = \frac{\langle p_{\mathrm{pred}}, p_{\mathrm{obs}}\rangle}{\|p_{\mathrm{pred}}\| \cdot \|p_{\mathrm{obs}}\| + \epsilon}, \quad R = \mathrm{mean}(\mathrm{ncc}_0)$$

6. NCC_maxlag Single

Zhang et al. 2019, Geophysics
$$R = \mathrm{mean}\!\left(\max_{\tau \in [-80,80]} \mathrm{CC}(\tau) - \lambda \cdot |\tau^*|\right), \quad \lambda = 0.005$$

7. Envelope_NCC Single

Oh & Alkhalifah 2018, GJI
$$e = |\mathcal{H}(p)|, \quad R = \mathrm{NCC}(e_{\mathrm{pred}}, e_{\mathrm{obs}})$$

Hilbert envelope → NCC. Produces artificial low frequencies for background velocity recovery.

8. AWI — Adaptive Waveform Inversion Single

Warner & Guasch 2016, Geophysics
$$W(\omega) = \frac{P_{\mathrm{obs}} \cdot P_{\mathrm{pred}}^*}{|P_{\mathrm{pred}}|^2 + \epsilon}, \quad w[t] = \mathrm{IFFT}(W)$$ $$R = -\frac{\langle |t| \cdot w[t]^2 \rangle}{\langle w[t]^2 \rangle}$$

Wiener matching filter time-spread penalty. If velocity correct, w ≈ δ(t).

9. Phase_func Single Failed

Wenzhe (proposed)
$$X = \mathrm{FFT}(x),\; X_{\mathrm{norm}} = X/|X|,\; x_{\mathrm{phase}} = \mathrm{IFFT}(X_{\mathrm{norm}}),\; R = -\|x_{\mathrm{phase}}^{\mathrm{pred}} - x_{\mathrm{phase}}^{\mathrm{obs}}\|^2$$

Implemented correctly per specification but fundamentally fails (mean 210.8). Amplitude normalization destroys essential physical information.

10. TT — Travel-Time Single

Allen 1978 (STA/LTA); Luo & Schuster 1991 (WTI)
$$R = -|t_{\mathrm{first}}(p_{\mathrm{pred}}) - t_{\mathrm{first}}(p_{\mathrm{obs}})|$$

STA/LTA energy-ratio picker, GPU-batched PyTorch implementation.

▸ 8. Standard OT W₂ — Métivier et al. 2016 NEW

Wasserstein_W₂ — Quadratic Optimal Transport Training

Métivier et al. 2016, GJI
$$\tilde{s}(t) = \frac{s(t)^2 + \eta}{\int (s(t)^2 + \eta)\,dt}, \quad \eta = 10^{-3}$$ $$F(t) = \sum_{\tau \le t} \tilde{s}(\tau), \quad F^{-1}(\alpha) = \mathrm{searchsorted}(F, \alpha)$$ $$W_2^2 = \frac{1}{Q}\sum_{k=1}^{Q} \big(F^{-1}_{\mathrm{pred}}(\alpha_k) - F^{-1}_{\mathrm{obs}}(\alpha_k)\big)^2, \quad Q=500$$ $$R = -\sum_{\mathrm{traces}} W_2$$
W₁ (CDF, existing)W₂ (Quantile, new)
Non-negativity$|d[t]|$ (absolute)$d[t]^2 + \eta$ (squared + baseline, Métivier)
Distance$W_1 = \sum|\Delta\mathrm{CDF}|$ (L1)$W_2^2 = \mathrm{mean}(|\Delta F^{-1}|^2)$ (L2)
Fidelity to Métivier 2016SimplifiedExact formula match
